About the family

The Siddhi Family

The Siddhi Guthi is the living thread of our family — a tradition passed from our forebears to bring relatives together for worship, festivals, and the quiet duties of kinship. The Guthi Ghar is where we gather to honour our deities, mark life's milestones, and remember those who came before us.

This portal is our private space: a single place to find family records, follow upcoming gatherings, view our heritage, and stay connected wherever each of us may live. It belongs to every member of the family.
